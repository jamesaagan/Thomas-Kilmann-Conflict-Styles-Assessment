Interpreting Your Scores

You may look at your scores and wonder how “right” they are. There are no right and wrong answers in this assessment, as it examines how you handle conflict situations which can vary greatly.

Different contexts call for different approaches, and there is a time and a place to use each of the conflict styles.

Note:

This quiz is based on the TKI instrument, published by CPP, Inc.—further information and TKI materials can be found on www.cpp.com.

Choose the statement that best describes you in a conflict situation:

How to read your scores (Range 0-12)

9 - 12 You tend to use this style frequently.
5 - 8 You use this style sometimes.
0 - 4 You don't use this style very often.
